**Verify retry is blocked in server logs** — after the call, you should NOT see any new dispatch attempts for `performance-review`, `ad-optimization`, or `growth-report`. The tasks will remain in their current status with `reviewFeedback` set to the hold marker.

## Rollback

The `pauseRetries` change sets `task.attempts = task.maxAttempts` and `task.reviewFeedback` to the hold string. To undo the hold on a specific task:

```bash
# Retry (which clears the hold and resets attempts to 0)
POST /api/tasks/<taskId>/retry
```

Or manually via the Orchestrator's `retryTask` method which resets `task.attempts = 0` and re-queues.
