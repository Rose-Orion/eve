## Diagnosis

The `showDeliverablePreview` function currently shows content for **all** deliverable types (Brand, Homepage, Products) regardless of whether the content is ready — the Brand path already has a "building" state, but the `onclick` handler is always attached to every deliverable card, making the button always clickable. More critically, the `viewFloorBuilding` function builds deliverable cards with `onclick="${d.onclick}"` on every card with no guard, so even cards with `⏳ Building` or `🔨 Building` status still invoke `showDeliverablePreview` which opens a modal. The fix has two parts: (1) make `showDeliverablePreview` check readiness and show a proper "not ready" state for Homepage/Products when the phase hasn't reached them, and (2) conditionally disable the `onclick` on deliverable cards whose content isn't ready to review.

---
