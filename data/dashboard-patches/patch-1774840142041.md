## Diagnosis

The `#owner-feedback-btn` is positioned `fixed` at `bottom: 24px; right: 24px`, which directly overlaps with the chat send button (`#chat-panel-send`) inside `#floor-chat-panel` — the send button sits in the bottom-right of the chat panel, and the bug report button floats on top of it. Moving the bug report button to the **top-right corner** (above the nav bar's z-index but clear of chat UI) and giving it a higher but non-conflicting position, or better: moving it to **bottom-left**, resolves the conflict entirely since the chat panel is anchored to the right side.

The cleanest fix: move it to `bottom: 24px; left: 24px` — the left side is always clear regardless of chat panel state.
