## Diagnosis

In `viewFloorBuilding()`, the "Report to EVE" section is rendered via an IIFE that returns a full panel including a section header, a glass container, a text input, a Send button, and feedback/auto-apply items. This entire block needs to be removed. The standalone `🐛` bug report button is injected globally in `init()` and is unrelated to this section — it will be unaffected.

## Patch
