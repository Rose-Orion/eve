Our philosophy

The label should
be the whole
story.

Most skincare brands treat transparency as a marketing claim — a sentence in their About section. At Vael, transparency is structural. Every ingredient is listed in plain language before you add to cart. Every efficacy percentage matches the published study it references.

We built Vael because the gap between lab and label had grown too wide. Proprietary blends hide what's inside. "Clinically tested" rarely says by whom. The word "clean" has no legal definition. We decided the simplest fix was also the most radical: just show everything.

Every ingredient, explained.

Niacinamide
2% concentration

Vitamin B3. Reduces inflammation, supports skin barrier function, and minimizes pore appearance. Published in the Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology, 2019.

Bakuchiol
2% concentration

Plant-derived retinol alternative. Comparable anti-aging results without photosensitivity. British Journal of Dermatology, 2019.

Squalane
Olive-derived

Lightweight emollient identical to human sebum. Non-comedogenic. Reinforces lipid barrier without occlusion.

Sodium Hyaluronate
Low molecular weight

Penetrates deeper than standard hyaluronic acid. Binds 1000x its weight in water within the dermis.

Cocamidopropyl Betaine
Primary surfactant

Coconut-derived cleanser. Gentle enough for eczema-prone skin. Maintains skin pH at 5.5.

Tocopherol
Vitamin E

Antioxidant that protects against free radical damage. Works synergistically with niacinamide for enhanced efficacy.
